About Us

Quality Meats.

Our Customer shave come to know us for the high quality and superior taste of all our meats. We make this consistency possible following the same three values we had when we first started in business.

We Only Source Organic Meat.

Whether it's Beef, Lamb, Pork or Chicken you can be assured all our cuts of Beef are free range. All our chickens are free range, our Beef and Lamb are grass fed and our Pork is all free range. The difference is in the taste. Come and see for yourself.

We Source From The Best Farms.

We have taken the time over the years to vet all our suppliers and their farms. We will only source fully traceable meat products, and ensure all meat comes from farms adhering to best farming practices.

we ensure you receive the freshest meat possible.

All our meat is fresh and never frozen. We take great care to ensure all our meats are of the highest standard when purchased. In addition, all our beef is 28 day aged to ensure it tastes excellent. You have the option to have your meat vacuum packed by us for storing and for sealing in that freshness.
