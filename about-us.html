a tradition born in history

Founded in 1958, KKMuttonstall began as a humble shop built on trust and quality, growing steadily as families returned for fresh, authentic cuts every day.

the early beginnings

In 1996, the legacy was passed to K.K. Noushad, who brought new vision and steady growth to the business. With strong dedication and evolving experience, he expanded our reach, opening the Alumoodu branch in 2000. Each step strengthened our foundation and helped us serve customers with the same trusted quality and care that shaped our journey.

our first store

Our journey began in 1958 at Murukumpuzha, where the very first KKMuttonstall shop opened its doors with a commitment to purity and honest service. From this single outlet, our reputation grew as families relied on us for fresh, carefully sourced cuts. Those early years shaped our identity, built customer trust, and laid the strong foundation that continues to guide every branch we open today.

growth through generations

From our early roots, each decade brought new challenges and opportunities that shaped who we are today. With steady expansion into Koithurkonam, Alumoodu, Attankulangara, and later Pothecode, our family worked tirelessly to maintain quality at every step. These branches grew from trust, tradition, and the loyal customers who supported us along the way. Every milestone strengthened our purpose and pushed us toward delivering excellence without compromise.

our legacy moving forwardy

As we continue to grow, our focus remains on delivering honest service, trusted quality, and the freshest cuts to every customer who walks through our doors. Each branch we opened strengthened our commitment to excellence, and every partnership we built pushed us to maintain higher standards. Today, we carry forward the same values that shaped our beginning, ensuring that our legacy stays alive for the generations to come.

FAQ

At KKMuttonstall, we’re always here to help with any questions you may have. Just give us a call or send us a message on WhatsApp — we’re happy to assist you anytime. Below are some of the common questions our customers ask.

Do all branches offer the same mutton cuts?

Yes, all branches follow the same cutting standards.

Can I place orders in advance?

Yes, you can call or WhatsApp to pre-order.

Do you provide home delivery?

Delivery depends on the branch. Please contact your nearest outlet.

What cuts do you offer?

We offer all standard cuts like curry cut, chops, ribs, leg pieces, and more.
